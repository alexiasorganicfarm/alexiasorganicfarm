About Our 5-Acre Tropical Farm

Alexia's Organic Farm is a vibrant 5-acre tropical farm in Beltangadi. Our journey began with a deep passion for the earth and a commitment to sustainable living.

Home to Jackfruit, Mango, Cinnamon, Jamun, Kokum, Coconut, and Cashew, with a pond and vegetable patches, it's a sanctuary for biodiversity and balance.

We invite you to experience the peace of nature and witness the magic of growth firsthand at our green haven.
