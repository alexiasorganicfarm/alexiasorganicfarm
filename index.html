About Our 5-Acre Tropical Farm

Alexia's Organic Farm is a vibrant 5-acre tropical farm nestled in the serene landscapes of Beltangadi, Dakshina Kannada. Our journey began with a deep passion for the earth and a commitment to sustainable living. We've poured our heart into cultivating this land, transforming it into a flourishing sanctuary where nature's bounty thrives.

Our farm is a celebration of biodiversity, home to majestic Jackfruit, sweet Mango, fragrant Cinnamon, and countless other treasures like Jamun, Kokum, Coconut, and Cashew. With a tranquil pond and flourishing vegetable patches, itâ€™s a living testament to the beauty and abundance that sustainable farming can achieve.

We invite you to step into our green haven. Walk among the trees, breathe in the fresh air, and discover the simple joys of connecting with the land. Alexia's Organic Farm is more than just a farm; it's a place where you can experience the profound peace of nature and witness the magic of growth firsthand.
