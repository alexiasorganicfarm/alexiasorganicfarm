🌿 Why Visit Alexia’s Organic Farm?

✨ A Place to Breathe, Not Just Be

Step into a space where silence is sacred, trees are teachers, and time slows down. Alexia’s Organic Farm isn’t a tourist spot—it’s a living forest in the making, designed to reconnect you with nature, simplicity, and yourself.

🌱 What You’ll Experience

  • Nature Immersion: Walk through native forest trails, sit by the pond, and listen to birdsong instead of traffic.
  • Organic Living: Learn how food is grown without chemicals, and maybe even help harvest seasonal fruits and vegetables.
  • Quiet Reflection: Bring your own lunch, find a shady tree, and enjoy uninterrupted time with your thoughts.
  • Sustainable Values: Every visit supports the soil, the trees, and the community—not profit.

🤝 How It Works

  • Visits are by appointment only—we keep it personal and peaceful.
  • We ask for a modest fixed contribution to help us care for the farm and keep this space peaceful for all visitors.
  • No commercial activities. No crowds. Just nature, and you.

Getting Here

Parking is available on-site. The farm is 2 km from the main road. You can take a rickshaw or walk.

What to Bring

  • Comfortable shoes

  • Insect repellent

Pets are welcome with a ₹50 entry fee. Please bring water bowls, poop bags, and keep your pet on a leash at all times.

Visitor Code of Conduct

Welcome to Alexia’s Organic Farm — a place to slow down, breathe in nature, and share in the peace our land offers. To ensure everyone enjoys their time here and that our natural environment stays healthy for years to come, we kindly ask you to follow these shared responsibilities:

Supervision of Minors

Children under 18 must be accompanied and supervised by a responsible adult or guardian at all times while on the farm. Visitors are fully responsible for the safety and behaviour of the minors in their care. Please be aware that the farm contains natural hazards such as ponds, uneven terrain, and wildlife. Alexia’s Organic Farm cannot be held liable for accidents or injuries caused by lack of supervision or negligence.

Respect for Nature & Each Other

Help us keep the farm a peaceful and welcoming space for everyone. Kindly avoid disruptive noise, littering, vandalism, or any behaviour that may disturb people, plants, or animals. Stay on marked paths and follow any posted guidance to protect both yourself and the farm’s ecosystem.

Privacy & Photography

We encourage photography of the farm’s landscapes, plants, and wildlife — but please respect the privacy of other visitors. Do not take photos or videos of individuals without their consent.

Safety First

Please be mindful of your surroundings and follow all safety signs. Do not touch or damage plants, trees, or farm equipment. Wildlife should be observed from a safe distance.

Entry Contribution

Entry is ₹50 per person for visitors aged 18 and above. Visitors under 18 may enter free of charge when accompanied by an adult. Your contribution supports the care, maintenance, and conservation of the farm.

Thank you for helping us keep Alexia’s Organic Farm safe, beautiful, and peaceful for all who visit. 🌱
