Frequently Asked Questions

Our Vision & Practices

Q: What is the "forest dream"?
A: The "forest dream" is our long-term vision to transform our 5-acre land into a thriving, self-sustaining forest garden. We are working to create a diverse ecosystem with a wide variety of native trees, fruit trees, and medicinal plants, all cultivated using organic and permaculture principles.

Q: Do you use pesticides or chemical fertilizers?
A: No, we are a completely organic farm. We are dedicated to sustainable farming practices and do not use any chemical pesticides, herbicides, or synthetic fertilizers. We believe in working with nature, not against it, to produce healthy and wholesome produce.

Q: Can we visit the farm?
A: Yes, we welcome visitors! We offer guided tours to explain our farming methods and the vision behind our forest dream. You can learn more about our visiting hours and book a tour through our contact page.

Q: What kind of produce do you grow?
A: We cultivate a wide range of produce, including seasonal fruits, vegetables, and spices. Our focus is on growing native and traditional varieties that are well-suited to the local climate.

Q: Do you offer farm stays?
A: While we don't currently offer traditional farm stays, we are exploring options for immersive workshops and retreats in the future. Please follow our updates on social media or subscribe to our newsletter for the latest information.
